The Tension

You've built something most people would envy. The title, the compensation, the track record. And yet there's a quiet dissonance — the sense that what got you here isn't what will carry you forward.

Maybe AI is reshaping your industry faster than your org can adapt. Maybe you've been performing at the top of a game you no longer want to win. Maybe you know exactly what you want to do next — you just haven't had the structure or support to actually do it.

This isn't burnout. This is a signal.

The Role Has Changed

AI and market shifts are rewriting job descriptions overnight. What made you indispensable last year may not matter next year.

The Optimization Trap

You keep refining a career that no longer reflects who you're becoming. More efficiency in the wrong direction is still the wrong direction.

The Readiness Gap

You don't need motivation. You need a structured path from where you are to where you know you should be — with someone who's guided this transition before.

About

Hina Patel Franklin

I'm a Licensed Clinical Social Worker and executive coach based in Woodland Hills, California. I work with high-performing professionals who are navigating career transitions, leadership identity shifts, and the particular kind of restlessness that comes from being good at something you've outgrown.

My approach combines clinical depth with strategic pragmatism. I don't believe in open-ended exploration for people who think in systems and timelines. I believe in structured, high-impact work that honors both the emotional complexity of change and the executive instinct to move with purpose.

Before private practice, I worked across clinical and organizational settings — building a perspective that lives at the intersection of psychological insight and professional performance.
